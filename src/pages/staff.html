Head Teacher (ECE) Sarah Oliver

Since graduating from the University of Guelph, Ontario, with a Bachelor of Applied Science in Child Studies, Sarah has had over 15 years of experience working with children, families and care providers in a variety of settings including Waldorf schools, non-profit organizations, preschools, childcare centres and summer camps.

Sarah started the After-School Care Program at Trillium Waldorf School in Guelph and was a frequent supply teacher in the Parent and Child program, kindergarten, and grades 1 through 8. At Waldorf Academy in Toronto, she was the Lead Teacher of the Sky Garden Preschool Class at the Childcare Centre. In 2016 Sarah moved from Ontario to Victoria, and she definitely does not miss the snow. She enjoys making art, handicrafts, gardening, cooking, photography and spending time outdoors getting to know the plants and animals.
