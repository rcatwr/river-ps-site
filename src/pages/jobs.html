Employment Opportunities

Early Childhood Educator (ECE)

The River Preschool's program is based upon Waldorf pedagogy which encourages and nurtures children's sense of wonder in the world. It is an approach that creates a warm, loving and caring environment with an emphasis on developing kind and trusting relationships with others. The Waldorf philosophy lends itself beautifully to being integrated with a faith-based approach to teaching young children. The River Preschool will model reverence and thankfulness in daily activities. The festivals and seasons of the Christian calendar will be followed and celebrated. The River Preschool will be in the St. Barnabas Anglican Church Hall at 1525 Begbie Street, Victoria, BC.

Hours for ECE: 28/hours per week. 10 months/year
ECE Wages: $25.08/hour which includes benefits for CPP, EI and vacation pay.
Start Date: August 26th, 2019

The successful candidate will:

  • embrace and implement the philosophy of The River Preschool as described above.
  • work as a strong team member with the director and with the St. Barnabas community life minister.
  • build strong relationships with the parents and help them to understand the philosophy of The River Preschool.
  • be able to plan, develop and implement a curriculum in which the hours of the day and the seasons of both the natural year and the church year are marked and celebrated in a predictable, rhythmic manner.
  • have strong interpersonal relationship and communication skills.
  • have a valid ECE license to practice in BC; a satisfactory criminal record check; provide documentation of their immunization and TB status; a valid First Aid/CPR certificate.

Responsibilities

  • curriculum development and teaching.
  • daily preparation and clean-up; involve the children in food preparation and serving and in tidying up play areas.
  • attendance at regular staff meetings.
  • supervision of ECEA and helper.
  • parent communication and parent volunteer coordination.
  • organize and coordinate celebrations and festivals with the Community Life Minister of St. Barnabas.
  • organization and ordering of resources in conjunction with the director.

Related experience in a Waldorf-inspired playgroup, preschool, or other Waldorf community program would be preferred. In addition, involvement in a Christian community would also be a significant benefit. Further training in Waldorf pedagogy can be discussed with the successful candidate.

Please submit an application form to:
riverpreschoolvic@gmail.com
In the subject line write: Application to B. Stacy (Secretary)

A cover letter, resume and copies of relevant certificates may be submitted to the above email address.

ECEA/Assistant Educator

It is an approach that creates a warm, loving and caring environment with an emphasis on developing kind and trusting relationships with others. The Waldorf philosophy lends itself beautifully to being integrated with a faith-based approach to teaching young children. Hours for ECEA: 20 hours/week; 10 months/year.
ECEA Wages: $20.00/hour which includes benefits for CPP, EI and vacation pay.
Start Date: September 3, 2019
Requirements: An ECEA certificate, a current first aid certificate, a clear criminal reference check, immunization and TB status.

The assistant educator will work with the director, head teacher, helper and other church staff to deliver a preschool program based upon Waldorf pedagogy and rooted in the faith and practices of the St. Barnabas church community.

The successful candidate for the assistant educator position will:

  • Support the lead teacher in designing and implementing the daily preschool program.
  • Learn, with the support of the director and head teacher, and use a Waldorf-based approach with regard to rhythm, behavior guidance, and other classroom practices.
  • Build warm relationships with the children's parents and families.
  • Perform prep and clean-up work as required.
  • Assist the ECE in organizing celebrations throughout the year.
  • Assist the ECE in maintaining administrative records as per VIHA guidelines.
