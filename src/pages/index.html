Welcome!

The River Preschool is a Christian faith-based, Waldorf-inspired preschool opening in September 2019 at St. Barnabas Anglican Church in Fernwood.

We are now taking contact information for families interested in enrolling for the fall. Please see our program and registration information.

For a decade St Barnabas church has been the home of many family programs including a Waldorf-inspired playgroup, after school Waldorf programs, craft programs, and a monthly family service.
