Typical Day

8:50 - 9:00 Arrival
  • Children arrive with their parent/care provider
  • Place belongings in cubby
  • Wash hands
  • Children are received by a teacher at the table to participate in the daily activity
9:00 – 10:00 Free Play
  • Children transition from the daily activity to free play when they are ready
  • Some children may choose to remain at the table longer and help with clean up and setting the table for snack
    Tidy Up
  • Children and teachers work together to tidy up the classroom
10:00 – 10:30 Washroom and Hand Washing Routine
  • Teachers support children with hygiene routine
    Snack
  • Children and teachers gather together at the table
  • Thanks is given for the food
  • Snack is served family style at the table
10:30 – 10:40 Rest and Story or Puppet Play
  • Children transition to the carpet/mats and settle into a place to rest
  • A simple, story/puppet play will be repeated each day for the whole week. The stories/plays will be selected to reflect the seasonal and festival calendar.
10:40 – 10:50 Circle
  • Movement, singing, verses, and finger plays.
  • Circles will be repeated for 2-3 weeks, allowing time for the children to learn the songs and verses. The songs and verses will be selected to reflect the seasonal and festival calendar.
10:50 – 11:55
    Dressing for Outside
    Walk to Stadacona Park for Outdoor Play
11:55 – 12:00 Closing Verse and Goodbye
  • Children and teachers will gather for a closing goodbye verse
  • Parents may be included in closing
  • Children will remain with the teachers until their parent/care provider pick them up from the park
